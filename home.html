 The JUP2 Interclass Sport's Day is a sports event held by JUP2 students under the subject "Sports and Management". The event lasted for a week, from 7th til 14th of November 2022. The tournaments held are volleyball, futsal, and badminton.

This website is made as a mini gallery to showcase some of the best photos taken during the sports event, and serving as a token of memory for this semester's happenings.
